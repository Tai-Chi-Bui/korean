### 6. **How to Form Syllables**

Korean syllables are formed by combining consonants and vowels. Typically, the structure is:
- Consonant + Vowel (e.g., 가, 나, 다)
- Consonant + Vowel + Consonant (e.g., 강, 밥, 먹)
